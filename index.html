"Welcome music lovers"

The concert of the century.
The most spectacular meeting of pop and rock in 2022.

A happy meeting with your favorite bands that will leave an unforgettable memory with a perfect sound that will reverberate throughout Colombia, only at the Arena Plaza in Bogota in October.

2022.10.21(FRI) - 22(SAT)

Bogotá, Cota, Cundinamarca

Main Program

  • Opening

    Inauguration of the event with the general director of the festival.

  • Musical History

    Here you will find the main milestones in the musical journey of our guest bands.

  • Store

    Only on this site can you buy souvenirs and musical products from your favorite band.

  • Main Event

    You can listen to all the bands Friday and Saturday from 4 pm and we will finish at 11 pm.

  • Closure

    Each of the bands in a special place will be autographing your favorite memories.
